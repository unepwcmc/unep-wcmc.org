Fellowships

We appoint exceptional individual scientists as honorary Fellows or Senior Fellows of UNEP-WCMC. This helps us access skills and advice of senior scientists, while providing our Fellows with unique professional opportunities.

Placements for MSc and MPhil Student Projects

We offer opportunities for students who are enrolled on a relevant university MSc or MPhil course to undertake their project work at the Centre. If you are interested in a student placement please contact recruitment@unep-wcmc.org

Scholarships for the Cambridge MPhil degree in Conservation Leadership

Since 2010, the University of Cambridge has been working with UNEP-WCMC to deliver an MPhil degree in Conservation Leadership. Details of the course and scholarship opportunities can be found here.

Volunteering opportunities

We regularly offer unpaid internships to those keen to strengthen their professional experience in the biodiversity sector. Please contact recruitment@unep-wcmc.org and include a CV and covering letter outlining your interest and availability.
