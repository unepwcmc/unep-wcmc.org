Enhance the knowledge base

Governments, businesses and communities need to know what environmental resources they have, where they are, and what state they are in. We bring together information large amounts of information about biodiversity and the way it is managed from around the world and share it with partners. The World Database on Protected Areas holds almost 200,000 records of marine and terrestrial protected areas, providing the knowledge base underpining many other initiatives to manage protected areas better.
